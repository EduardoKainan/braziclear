Our Story

BraziClean Brand Story

At BraziClean, our story is built on resilience, hard work, and a genuine desire to serve others.

BraziClean was founded by Rejane, a woman who came to the United States carrying more than dreams — she carried a lifetime of determination. Growing up in Brazil, she experienced firsthand the challenges that many families face: uncertainty, hardship, and the constant need to persevere. Those experiences taught her the value of dedication, integrity, and never giving up.

After building a new life in America alongside her husband, Rejane saw an opportunity to create something meaningful — a company that would do more than clean homes and businesses. She wanted to create a service that would bring peace of mind, comfort, and trust to every client.

That vision became BraziClean.

Today, BraziClean proudly serves families, professionals, businesses, and property owners throughout the Nashville area with detailed, personalized cleaning services. We believe that a clean environment is about more than appearance. It creates space for families to spend time together, professionals to focus on what matters most, and businesses to present their very best.

Every home and business we enter is treated with the same care, respect, and attention to detail we would give our own. We understand that inviting someone into your space requires trust, and we work every day to earn and keep that trust.

Our commitment is simple:

  • Deliver exceptional cleaning services.
  • Treat every client with honesty and respect.
  • Pay attention to the details others overlook.
  • Create healthier, more comfortable spaces.
  • Build lasting relationships through reliable service.

BraziClean is more than a cleaning company. It is the result of a journey fueled by faith, perseverance, and the belief that serving others well can make a meaningful difference.

Frequently Asked Questions

Questions Before Booking a Cleaning?

Clear answers to help you choose the right cleaning service for your home.

How much does residential cleaning cost?

The price depends on the size of your home, the type of cleaning, current condition, and how often you need service. BraziClean provides a personalized quote after understanding your needs.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

No. Many clients prefer to provide access instructions and return to a clean home. If you want to be present for the first visit, that works too.

Do you bring cleaning products and equipment?

Yes. Our team brings the products and equipment needed for the cleaning. If you prefer specific products for your home, let us know before the appointment.

Are you bonded and insured?

Yes. BraziClean is bonded and insured, giving you peace of mind while our team cares for your home or business.

How often should my home be professionally cleaned?

Most homes benefit from weekly or biweekly cleaning. Monthly service can work well for lighter maintenance, while deep cleaning is ideal for seasonal resets, move-in, or move-out needs.
